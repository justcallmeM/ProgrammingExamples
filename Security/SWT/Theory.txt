Simple Web Tokens (SWT)
	Description: SWT is a token format used to transfer information between parties and 
		is simpler compared to other token formats. SWTs are URL-encoded key-value pairs.
	Structure: Key-value pairs separated by ampersands (&) and then signed.

Simple Web Tokens (SWT)
	Pros:
		Simple: Easy to generate and validate.
		Compact: Smaller size compared to JWT.
	Cons:
		Deprecated: Not widely used anymore and considered less secure.
		Limited: Less powerful and flexible compared to JWT and OAuth2 tokens.
